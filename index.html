Planetary Explorer

Welcome to my small exploration project, a 3D interactive model of our solar system built to make learning about space a little more fun and engaging.

You can explore each planet, zoom in for a closer look, view real NASA information, discover how much you would weigh on different worlds, and compare planets side by side.

This project was created as a way to combine technology, design, and curiosity into something interactive and educational.

Developed by Jaymie
